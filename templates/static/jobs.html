Jobs at Sefaria

UX/UI Designer

The Sefaria Project is looking for our first full time designer to help create the best possible experience for textual engagement that the world has ever known.

About You

  • You're constantly struggling to make things simpler.
  • You can share with us a portfolio of products, experiences and graphics you've created and you can explain what motived the design of each.
  • Your best ideas stem from research; you know how to stage usability studies or conduct user interviews and observations to identify real needs ands pain points.
  • You think holistically about user needs and the full flow of user experience, but you're comfortable working with the fine details of pixels of palettes that make an application great.
  • You think text is beautiful. You could write a haiku about your favorite typeface.
  • You can read Hebrew and are comfortable designing layouts with Hebrew text.
  • You know how to validate your design decisions with data by crafting A/B tests and scrutinizing analytics.
  • You're an excellent communicator (in English) and value critical feedback and diverse opinions.

About the Role

  • You'll be the first person on our team working exclusively on design and you'll help set the priorities for time spent on new products and necessary redesigns.
  • You'll be working across a full spectrum of design levels (with a focus at the top):
    • High level product design for web and mobile.
    • Reusable interactions and widgets.
    • Visual components like graphics, icons, and palettes.
    • Informational layouts for communication.

Bonus Points

    • You've got a strong interest in and knowledge of Jewish textual learning.
    • You can talk for hours about the successes and failures of the Vilna Daf as a learning interface.
    • You're comfortable working in CSS and HTML when appropriate.
    • You like to hack out interactive designs yourself in Javascript.

Location

  • Remote OK, with a strong preference for San Francisco, New York or Jerusalem.

Compensation

  • Competitive Salary and Benefits.
