Jobs at Sefaria

Junior Content Engineer

Sefaria, the digital platform committed to making Jewish text accessible and evolvable, seeks a driven individual to help expand our library of text. This full-time, junior programmer will work on content-related tasks for our system. The role involves:

  • Analyzing structures of books and creating formal representations using Sefaria's toolset
  • Parsing text from various sources and loading them into Sefaria’s repository
  • Developing and improving tools used in content processing
  • Developing and improving algorithms used to find connections between text
  • Creating tools for non-technical power users to edit and manage large volumes of text

Key Responsibilities:

Sefaria’s database of text currently contains more than 54 million words and we’re working through a roadmap to triple the size of the library. Everything we obtain must be properly imported into our system. Our repository’s code base is Python running over MongoDB with a RESTful API. The process of parsing unstructured digital text generally involves analyzing the text to determine its structure, encoding that structure, writing custom scripts to convert the source text into the appropriate structure, and posting the resulting data to our server.


Desired Experience and Skills: Applicants need to understand the structure of Torah literature (http://www.sefaria.org/texts), and should have a background in programming and a history of learning Torah.
  • At least one year of programming experience
  • Python (or comparable experience)
  • Regular expressions and fuzzy matching
  • Knowledge of Judaic text (e.g., Torah, Talmud, Kabbalistic works, halachic works, commentaries, etc.)
  • Understanding of written Hebrew
  • English (professional working proficiency)

Location: Preference for Jerusalem/Israel; Remote OK.

To apply, please send your resume to jobs@sefaria.org. If you have a GitHub account, please include a link on your resume.
