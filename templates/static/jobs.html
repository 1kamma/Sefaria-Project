Education Associate

The Education Associate will support the development of Sefaria’s work with Jewish educators, oversee the logistics for a growing number of partnerships with schools and organizations, imagine and help create engaging educational content, and teach others how to do so using Sefaria.

PROGRAM SUMMARY:

Sefaria’s Education team engages a growing network of Jewish educators in a variety of settings—day schools, supplementary schools, youth groups, young adult and adult learning programs—across the United States. For the 2016-17 school year, the Education Team will partner with 16 day schools and 8 Jewish organizations across the United States. Our goals are to:

  • Introduce Sefaria as a resource that can provide access to texts and translations, change and improve pedagogy around Jewish texts, reduce teacher prep time, and provide students with new outlets for exploration and research.
  • Continue to develop Sefaria in response to feedback and ideas from users, especially educators.

This initiative will help us explore best practices for teaching with Sefaria with the goal of improving pedagogy around Jewish texts and fostering innovation in Jewish education.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

Sefaria’s School and Institutional Partnership Initiative

  • Manage the logistics and operations of the initiative (20+ partners), including but not limited to scheduling meetings, trainings, site visits and providing educators with material needs.
  • Maintain relationships and regular communication with partner educators.  
  • Document and track Sefaria’s interactions with partner educators.

Engaging and Training Educators

  • Help seek out new opportunities to expand Sefaria’s educational influence by connecting with educators and educational organizations.
  • Manage Facebook page for educators: encourage dialogue, respond to queries, etc.
  • Write and share case studies from our partner educators doing innovative work with Sefaria.  
  • Coordinate and facilitate live and/or online Sefaria training sessions.
  • Arrange opportunities for Sefaria Field Experts (Sefaria’s educational advisory board) to meet with key team members.
  • Perform research as requested by the Director of Education
  • Assist with grants and grant reports as they relate to Sefaria’s education work.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Background in education.
  • High level of comfort working with technology and experience working with educational technology is a plus.
  • Exemplary organizational and time management skills, in order to coordinate a variety of fast-moving projects.
  • Willingness to accept new and varying assignments and remain flexible, with a profound sense of responsibility.
  • Strong interpersonal skills with a passion for and commitment to being part of a team-oriented, mission-driven organizational culture.
  • Proactive and self-driven worker with the ability to troubleshoot and solve problems.
  • Excellent writing skills.
  • Bachelor's degree.
  • At least 2-4 years of professional post-college work experience.

Travel: Occasional travel and non-traditional hours required.

Supervision: Reports to the Director of Education (based in Chicago).

Location: New York or Chicago preferred.

Salary: $50k+, commensurate with experience.

To apply: Please send a brief cover letter and resume to jobs@sefaria.org.

Junior Content Engineer

עברית בהמשך

Sefaria, the open digital platform for Jewish texts, seeks a driven individual to help expand our library of text. This full-time, junior programmer will work on content-related tasks for our system. The role involves:

  • Analyzing structures of texts and creating formal representations using Sefaria's toolset
  • Parsing text from various sources and loading them into Sefaria’s repository
  • Developing and improving tools used in content processing
  • Developing and improving algorithms used to find connections between different texts
  • Creating tools for non-technical power users to edit and manage large volumes of text

Key Responsibilities:

Sefaria’s database currently contains more than 60 million words. We’re working through a roadmap to triple the size of the library by the end of 2018. Everything we obtain must be properly imported into our system. Our repository’s code base is Python running over MongoDB with a RESTful API. The process of parsing unstructured digital text generally involves analyzing the text to determine its structure, encoding that structure, writing custom scripts to convert the source text into the appropriate structure, and posting the resulting data to our server.


Desired Experience and Skills: Applicants need to understand the structure of Torah literature (http://www.sefaria.org/texts), and should have a background in programming and a history of learning Torah.
  • At least one year of programming experience
  • Python (or comparable experience)
  • Regular expressions and fuzzy matching
  • Knowledge of Judaic text (e.g., Torah, Talmud, Kabbalistic works, halakhic works, commentaries, etc.)
  • Understanding of written Hebrew
  • English (professional working proficiency)
  • Detail oriented
  • Proactive worker and a self-starter

Location: Preference for Jerusalem/Israel; Remote acceptable.

Job Type: This is a full time permanent position.

To apply, please send your resume to jobs@sefaria.org. If you have a GitHub account, please include a link on your resume.

מתכנת תוכן זוטר

ספאריה, הפלטפורמה הפתוחה לטקסטים יהודיים, מחפשת מישהו או מישהי עם מוטיבציה שיעזרו להרחיב את ספריית הטקסטים שלנו. מדובר בתפקיד משרת מתכנת זוטר במשרה מלאה שיעסוק בתוכן עבור המערכת שלנו. התפקיד כולל:

  • אנליזה של מבנה הטקסט ויצירת ייצוגים פורמליים באמצעות סט הכלים שלנו.
  • כריית טקסטים ממקורות שונים למרכיביהם והטענתם למאגר של ספאריה.
  • פיתוח ושיפור כלים לעיבוד תוכן.
  • פיתוח ושיפור אלגוריתמים למציאת קשרים בין טקסטים שונים.
  • יצירת כלים למשתמשים לא-טכניים לעריכה ולניהול כמות גדולה של טקסט.

תחומי אחריות:

מסד הנתונים של ספאריה כרגע מכיל יותר משישים מליון מילים. אנו כרגע עובדים לקראת הכפלת גודל הספריה פי שלוש עד סוף 2018. צריך להזין למאגר את כל החומרים שאנו משיגים בצורה הנכונה. המאגר שלנו כתוב בפייתון ורץ על MongoDB עם RESTful API. תהליך כריית הטקסט הדיגיטלי כרוך באנליזה של הטקסט כדי לגלות את המבנה שלו, קידוד המבנה, כתיבת סקריפטים להמרת טקסט המקור למבנה המיועד, והעלאת התוצאה לשרת שלנו.


ניסיון וכישורים דרושים: המועמדים צריכים להבין את המבנה של טקסטים מהתורה (http://www.sefaria.org/texts), וצריכים רקע בתכנות ובלימוד תורה.

  • לפחות שנה אחת של ניסיון בתכנות.
  • פייתון (או ניסיון דומה).
  • Regular expressions and fuzzy matching.
  • ידע בטקסטים יהודיים (למשל תורה, תלמוד, קבלה, הלכה, מפרשים ועוד).
  • יכולת קריאה בעברית מן המקורות.
  • אנגלית ברמת שפת אם

מקום עבודה: עדיפות לירושלים/ישראל; ניתן לעבוד מרחוק.
