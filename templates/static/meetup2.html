Sefaria.org NYC Meetup #2

Wednesday, January 16th 2013 at 8pm


Please join us for our second NYC meetup - open to Sefaria contributors, supporters and anyone with an interest in Torah and technology.

Come to support our work, but also to meet other great people interested in sharing Jewish learning online.

Agenda:
  1. Previewing new features we've been working on.
  2. Sharing difficult design and textual issues we're facing, to get your input.
  3. Listening to your opinions on what's working with the site, what isn't, and what you wish we'd do next.
  4. General fantasizing about the future of Jewish learning.
